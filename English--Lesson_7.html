Тема:
Past Continuous Tense. Review.
Прошедшее Продолжительное Время. Повтор.

Формула для wh- вопросов:
Wh- + was/were + subject + verb (-ing) + when\while <additional sentence> + time

Пример:
Wh-y + were + they + having rest + while + <everybody else were working> + last week?

Упражнения:

Affirmative (+):
 While network load was rising, the hardware was heating up.
Air conditioning was working when I came to office.

Negative (-):
 While network load was rising, the hardware was not heating up. (variant 1)
While network load was not rising, the hardware was heating up. (variant 2)
While network load was not rising, the hardware was not heating up. (variant 3)
Air conditioning wasn't working when I came to office.

Interrogative (?):
Was the hardware heating up while the network load was rising? (variant 1)
Was the network load rising while the hardware was heating up? (variant 2)
Why were they having rest while everybody else were working?
Where were you excercising when you had holidays?
