Тема:
Future Tense.
Будущее Время.

Формула:

Will:
(+): subject + will + verb + time
(-): subject + will not (won't) + verb + time
(?): will + subject + verb + time
(wh): wh- + will + subject + verb + time

Going to:
(+): subject + am/is/are + going to + verb + time
(-): subject + am/is/are + not + going to + verb + time
(?): am/is/are + subject + going to + verb + time
(wh): wh- + am/is/are + subject + going to + verb + time

Случаи:

Affirmative (+):
 I will learn English next week.
She is going to participate in that discussion.

Negative (-):
 I won't (will not) learn English next week.
She isn't (is not) going to participate in that discussion.

Interrogative (?):
 Will I learn English next week?
Is she going to participate in that discussion?

Детали:
may, will, shall - modal verbs
I'm going to go to the shop == I'm planning to go to the shop.
shall - polite version of will; возможно

Приоритет строгости модальных глаголов:
1. have to
2. must
3. should
