Тема:
Present Simple Tense
Настоящее Простое время

Основное базовое время
Все повествовательные местоимения

Формула:

(+): subject + verb + time
(-): subject + do/does not + verb + time
(?): do/does + subject + verb + time

Случаи:
Affirmative (+):
 I work (every day)
You work (every morning)
He/She/It works (each week)
We work
They work

A little test: https://agendaweb.org/exercises/verbs/present/third-person

Negative (-):
 I don't work
You don't work
He/She/It doesn't work
We don't work
They don't work

A little test: https://agendaweb.org/exercises/verbs/present/interrogative-forms-pronouns

Interogative (?):
 Do I work?
Do you work?
Does he/she/it work?
Do we work?
Do they work?

A little test: https://agendaweb.org/exercises/verbs/present/interrogative-forms-pronouns
