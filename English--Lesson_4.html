Тема:
Past Simple Tense.
Простое Прошедшее Время.

Формула:

(+): subject + verb (-ed) (or II verb) + time
(-): subject + did not + verb + time
(?): did + subject + verb + time

Случаи:

Affirmative (+):
 I went home.
I finished my job yesterday.

Negative (-):
 I didn't go home.
I didn't finish my job yesterday. Did I?

Interrogative (?):
 Did I go?
Did I finish my job yesterday? Didn't you?
Did you eat my cookie?
Didn't you eat my cookie?
