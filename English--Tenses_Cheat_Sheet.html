Tenses Cheat Sheet


Present Simple
(+): subject + verb + time
(-): subject + do/does not + verb + time
(?): do/does + subject + verb + time
(wh): wh- do/does + subject + verb + time

Past Simple Tense
(+): subject + verb (-ed) (or II verb) + time
(-): subject + did not + verb (-ed) (or II verb) + time
(?): did + subject + verb (-ed) (or II verb) + time
(wh): wh- + did + subject + verb (-ed) (or II verb) + time

Present Continuous
(+): subject + am/is/are + verb (-ing) + time
(-): subject + am/is/are + not + verb (-ing) + time
(?): am/is/are + subject + verb (-ing) + time
(wh): wh- + am/is/are + verb (-ing) + time

Past Continuous Tense
 (+): subject + was/were + verb (-ing) + time
(-): subject + wasn't/weren't + verb (-ing) + time
(?): was/were + subject + verb (-ing) + time
(wh): wh- + was/were + subject + verb (-ing) + when\while <additional sentence> + time
