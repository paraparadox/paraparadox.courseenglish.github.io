"The Right Way of Leading"

1. Introduction
In the past when our company was in big troubles, we all fell in sad. Obviously, we were ready to give up. Fortunately, we had a big motivation and stimulation source - our director, our leader.
Many years passed from that times, however, I still remember one of the most important days in our company's life.

2. Main part
This is our director's speech: "... I bet this economic burst will cost us very much, there is no doubt in this. Hence, we will need to cut a part of our contracts. In addition, we cannot let the crisis to hit our clients and hurt their businesses. As usual, we are going to put our clients priority at the first place, similarly, as we are doing since first years of our company.
As well as if you read the history of our company, you will find that in such difficult situations, assuredly, we plan in the best way, we set the goals, and also we try to shut all the risks. As a rule, we beat ourselves each time!
In any case, remember that all these problems come to test us, in addition, to make us stronger. Therefore, we need to try harder to ran out from next crisis and become the best ones in our business.
Most importantly, I need you to bring us the victory and glory, to build a giant empire! ..."

3. Conclusion
Afterward, with these words our director signed a contract to buy a new startup, to make a bigger business. We caught him very inspired that day. He believed that we are dealing the best way. And certainly, he was right! :)
