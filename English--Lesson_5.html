Тема:
Past Continuous Tense.
Прошедшее Продолжительное Время.

Формула:

(+): subject + was/were + verb (-ing) + time
(-): subject + wasn't/weren't + verb (-ing) + time
(?): was/were + subject + verb (-ing) + time

Случаи:

Affirmative (+):
 I was watching TV when she called me.
She was cooking when I came home.
My brother was exercising for 6 months.
Jovid was coding when I wrote him.
When we finished our tasks our manager was testing the soft.
I was reading untill I falled asleep.

Negative (-):
 I was not watching TV when she called me.
She was not cooking when I came home.
My brother was not exercising for 6 months.
Jovid was not coding when I wrote him.
When we finished our tasks our manager was not testing the soft.
I was not reading untill I falled asleep.

Interrogative (?):
 Was I watching TV when she called?
Was she cooking when I came home?
Was my brother exercising for 6 months?
Was Jovid coding when I wrote him?
When we finished our tasks Was our manager testing the soft?
Was I reading untill I falled asleep?
